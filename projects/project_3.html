I have participated in research projects focused on analyzing clinical, molecular, and demographic factors influencing the survival and therapeutic response of oncology patients. My responsibilities included evaluating gene expression and specific mutations, identifying their impact on 5- and 10-year survival rates, as well as on treatment efficacy. I have analyzed the association between clinicopathological parameters (age, sex, ethnicity) and characteristics such as genetic mutations, tumor recurrence, and adverse effects, determining population subgroups with differential responses to therapies. To achieve this, I have applied advanced statistical methodologies, such as Fisher's exact test for associations in small samples, logistic regression to model relationships between binary variables and multiple factors, and survival analysis techniques (Kaplan-Meier, Cox models, and log-rank tests) to assess the impact of clinical and molecular factors on prognosis. These analyses have enabled the identification of predictive profiles and the optimization of personalized therapeutic strategies.
