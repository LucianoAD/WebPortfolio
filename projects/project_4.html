During my training in biomedical research, I contributed to the design and execution of wet lab experiments.
I gained hands-on experience in techniques such as:

  • Cell culture and microbiology
  • Flow cytometry measurements
  • RT-PCR
  • Western blot
  • Handling of murine models
Additionally, I learned how to analyze and process data from preclinical studies, particularly in the field of oncology. I transformed raw data (from cell viability assays, Western blots, and PCR) into structured datasets, applying statistical analyses like ANOVA and t-tests using tools such as GraphPad Prism and R to validate biological findings.
To effectively communicate results, I created clear visualizations (dose-response curves, boxplots, scatterplots) to support interpretation by multidisciplinary teams, and I also contributed to manuscript writing. This experience strengthened my data analysis skills, particularly in translating complex information into actionable insights and managing experimental data with methodological rigor and attention to detail.
